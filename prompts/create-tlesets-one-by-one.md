(Include this block at the beginning of EVERY prompt)

Hard constraints:
- Canvas size MUST be exactly 32x32 pixels.
- Output: PNG with transparent background.
- Pixel-perfect, no scaling, no padding, no extra border or margin.
- Top-down view tile for a 2D game (Stardew Valley-like readability).
- Limited palette (2–3 main colors + 1 accent).
- No text, no numbers, no UI elements.
- No glowing runes, no modern objects, no rails or mechanical tech.
- Style: carved dwarf stone, solemn, ancient (Khazad-dûm-inspired).
- Runes should be carved and worn (static, no glow).
- Keep contrast readable at 1:1 scale.
